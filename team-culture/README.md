## üè¢ Our Team Structure

Solution8 operates with a flat, collaborative structure designed to maximize agility and empowerment:

### Cross-Functional Teams
We organize around products and outcomes rather than functions. Each team typically includes:
- Software Engineers (Frontend, Backend, Full-Stack)
- QA/Test Engineers
- Product Managers
- UX/UI Designers
- DevOps Engineers

### Leadership Philosophy
We believe in **servant leadership**‚Äîleaders exist to support teams, remove blockers, and create conditions for success. Decision-making is pushed to those closest to the work.

### Collaboration Model
- **Autonomy**: Teams own their domain and decisions
- **Alignment**: Regular sync points ensure we're moving in the same direction
- **Accountability**: Clear ownership and transparent results
